The Glamping at HELP Resort

What is Glamping?

Each glamping has canvas wall, an wooden floor, a roof dome that can be opened, and a private deck

How are glamping furnished?

Each glamping is furnished with a gas fireplace, king-size bed with down quilt, comfy counch, dining table and chairs, mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoues and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
